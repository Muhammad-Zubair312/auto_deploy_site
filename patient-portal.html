Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the patient portal?

You can register by clicking the "Register here" link above or by contacting our office. We'll send you an invitation email with registration instructions.

Is my health information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal health information. Your privacy is our top priority.

Can I schedule appointments through the portal?

Yes, you can request appointments through the portal. Our staff will confirm your appointment and send you a notification once it's scheduled.

How quickly will I receive responses to messages?

Our healthcare team typically responds to portal messages within 1-2 business days. For urgent matters, please call our office directly.

Need Help Accessing Your Portal?

Our support team is available to assist you with any technical issues or questions about using the patient portal.
