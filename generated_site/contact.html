Frequently Asked Questions

How often are live scores updated?

Live scores are updated in real-time during matches. We have dedicated data feeds from official sources to ensure accuracy and timeliness.

Our statistics are compiled from official league data, verified sources, and our own analytics team to ensure comprehensive and accurate player metrics.
