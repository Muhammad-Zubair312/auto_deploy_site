Understanding Cricket Rankings

ICC Points System

Teams earn points based on match results. The rating is calculated by dividing total points by matches played.

Player Rankings

Individual player rankings consider runs scored, wickets taken, strike rates, and opposition strength.

Regular Updates

Rankings are updated after each completed match to reflect the most current performance data.
